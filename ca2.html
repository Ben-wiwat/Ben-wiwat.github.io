Capability area #2 — Develop high quality curricula and assessment

  1. Development of curriculum/program
    • In 2017, I was in charge of setting up the MSc in Financial Mathematics, which was submitted to the university course committee in June 2017. Details as shown on my 2017 Annual Review (page 3-4).
    • In 2020, I initiated the education collaboration between Curtin and Maejo University in Thailand and consequently developed a joint program of studies, leading to an MOU signed in April 2021. Details as shown on
      • Curtin-Maejo Student Articulation Pricipal Agreement (page 8)
      • Memorandum of Understanding for the Development of Academic and Research Collaboration (page 6)
  2. Assessment and Evaluation:
    I have designed assessments that are fair, valid, and reliable for all student with diverse cultural and academic background to ensure that they measure the intended learning outcomes. I am committed to upholding a high standard of educational quality in the courses I oversee. To that end, I frequently engage in peer review consultations with co-assessors to examine and enhance the quality of unit materials and assessments. I also carefully respond to student evaluations, utilising this feedback as a mechanism for continuous improvement in the units I manage.
