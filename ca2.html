  2. Impact on student learning

        I have designed assessments that are fair, valid, and reliable for all student with diverse cultural and academic background to ensure that they measure the intended learning outcomes. I am committed to upholding a high standard of educational quality in the courses I oversee. To that end, I frequently engage in peer review consultations with co-assessors to examine and enhance the quality of unit materials and assessments. I also carefully respond to student evaluations, utilising this feedback as a mechanism for continuous improvement in the units I manage. The impact of my teaching on students' learning can be seen from students' comments from the eVALUate report from 2019-2023. These comments attest to my expertise, teaching prowess, adaptability, and enthusiasm.

  3. Industry engagement

        My engagement activities over the past year have been characterised by collaborations with various government bodies and industry partners including Main Roads Western Australia, Roads and Maritime Services NSW and Sustainable Built Environment, National Research Centre Australia. By doing so, I ensure that students gain firsthand experience to real-world challenges and practical applications in their chosen field of study. This hands-on experience not only enriches their academic journey but also equips them with valuable skills and insights that are directly transferable to their future careers. It acts as a bridge between academic knowledge and industry practices, enhancing their preparedness for success in their chosen professions.
